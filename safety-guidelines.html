Safety Guidelines & Requirements

Review all rules before submitting your application. Rules are divided into general rules that apply to all permits, and specific rules per permit type.

General Rules — Apply to All Permit Types

Category Level Rule
Age Required Applicant must be 18 years of age or older.
Identity Required A valid national ID or passport must be submitted and must not be expired.
Documents Required All submitted documents must be in English or accompanied by an official certified translation.
Legal Required Applicant must have no pending legal violations or outstanding fines within the Smart City jurisdiction.
Legal Important Falsifying any information in the application will result in a permanent ban and referral for legal action.
Validity Important Renewal must be initiated before the permit expiry date. Operating with an expired permit is a violation.
Processing Info Processing times vary by permit type. Incomplete applications will not be processed.

Labor Permit — Specific Rules

Category Level Rule
Age Required Applicant must be 21 years of age or older at the time of application.
Age Required Workers under 18 years of age are strictly prohibited from being listed on this permit.
Documents Required Proof of employment or a labor contract signed by both parties.
Documents Required Medical fitness certificate issued within the last 3 months.
Legal Required Employer must be a registered legal entity within the Smart City jurisdiction.
Validity Info Permit is valid for 12 months. Renewal must be initiated at least 30 days before expiry.
Processing Info Standard processing time is 5–7 business days after a complete submission.

Construction Equipment Permit — Specific Rules

Category Level Rule
Age Required Primary operator must be at least 21 years old and hold a valid certified operator license.
Age Required Any secondary authorized user listed on the permit must be 18 years of age or older.
Documents Required Equipment registration certificate and valid insurance documents.
Documents Required Site authorization letter issued by the project owner or site supervisor.
Documents Important Safety inspection report dated within the last 6 months is mandatory.
Legal Required Third-party liability insurance must be active before permit issuance.
Legal Important Unauthorized equipment operation on-site will result in immediate confiscation and permit revocation.
Validity Info Permit is valid for the project duration, maximum 24 months. Extensions require full re-application.
Processing Info Standard processing time is 7–10 business days after submission.

Medical Device Permit — Specific Rules

Category Level Rule
Age Required Applicant must be 21 years of age or older and hold a valid medical professional license.
Age Required Minors are not permitted to operate or be listed as device custodians under any circumstance.
Documents Required Valid medical license and current professional registration certificate.
Documents Required Device manufacturer compliance and certification documents (FDA, CE, or SFDA approval).
Documents Required Facility license for the premises where the device will be stored and operated.
Documents Important Import clearance certificate is required if the device is sourced internationally.
Legal Required Device must carry approval from the National Medical Devices Authority prior to any use.
Legal Required Any adverse event or device malfunction must be reported within 72 hours of occurrence.
Validity Info Permit is valid for 24 months. An annual compliance audit is required to maintain active standing.
Processing Info Processing time is 10–14 business days due to mandatory regulatory review.

Electronic Device Permit — Specific Rules

Category Level Rule
Age Required Applicant must be 18 years of age or older for personal use permits.
Age Important Commercial deployment applicants must be 21+ and hold a valid business license.
Documents Required Device technical specification sheet including frequency band and power output documentation.
Documents Required Type approval certificate issued by the Communications Regulatory Authority.
Documents Important Cybersecurity compliance declaration is required for all IoT or network-connected devices.
Legal Required Device must operate within approved frequency ranges and must not cause interference to other systems.
Legal Required Any device collecting personal or location data must comply with the Smart City Data Privacy Act.
Legal Important Devices used for surveillance require a separate Security Permit before deployment.
Validity Info Permit is valid for 12 months. Any hardware or firmware modification immediately voids the permit.
Processing Info Standard processing time is 3–5 business days after a complete submission.
